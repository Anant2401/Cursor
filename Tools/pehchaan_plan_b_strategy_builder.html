⚡ Why Plan B Is Actually Plan A for Smart Students

In Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities, many students spend years preparing while earning little on the side. The ones who also pick up paid skills along the way often channel the same reading, discipline, and practice into work the market pays for — not because they are “smarter,” but because they noticed those habits have real value.
