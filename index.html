Pehchaan Careers is built for students in Korba, Bilaspur, Raigarh and Champa — Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities where metro-style counselling rarely reaches.

Why local guidance matters here

Families often hear generic advice — “take Science,” “prepare for government exams,” “move to a big city” — without anyone mapping what that means for fees, exams, or first jobs. Pehchaan speaks to that gap: honest trade-offs, clear next steps, and tools you can use from home before you commit lakhs to a course.

Why Pehchaan Exists

"I finished 12th — and nobody told me what to actually do next."

Anant Tripathi started at HCL Technologies as a data analyst — after a degree in Computer Engineering from Bilaspur, a wrong stream choice after Class 10, and no campus placement. Over the next 14 years he built his career entirely on his own, working across HCL, Goldman Sachs, Cognizant, and Acuity Analytics, where he now leads teams in business operations, automation, and sales enablement for global financial clients.

None of that came with a guide. Every choice, every city, every pivot — figured out alone. Pehchaan Careers is his answer to that silence: real, honest guidance for students from Korba, Bilaspur, Raigarh and Champa who are just as capable as anyone from a metro — but have never had someone sit with them and say, "Here is how it actually works."
