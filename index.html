About Pehchaan Careers

Pehchaan Careers is built to help young learners make confident choices. We focus on understanding each student’s strengths, interests, and aspirations, then map them to a clear and actionable path.

The Career Reality Students Face

Many students work hard, but never receive clear guidance about what choices lead to what jobs. Pehchaan exists to close that gap with practical, honest mentoring.
