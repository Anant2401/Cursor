Why Pehchaan Was Started

"I finished 12th — and nobody told me what to actually do next."

Anant Tripathi started at HCL Technologies as a data analyst — after a degree in Computer Engineering from Bilaspur, a wrong stream choice after Class 10, and no campus placement. Over the next 14 years he built his career entirely on his own, working across HCL, Goldman Sachs, Cognizant, and Acuity Analytics, where he now leads teams in business operations, automation, and sales enablement for global financial clients.

None of that came with a guide. Every choice, every city, every pivot — figured out alone. Pehchaan Careers is his answer to that silence: real, honest guidance for students from Korba, Bilaspur, Raigarh and Champa who are just as capable as anyone from a metro — but have never had someone sit with them and say, "Here is how it actually works."

Career Assessment Test

Free · Takes ~20 Minutes · No Login Required

Discover the career path that is built for you

Most students choose a career based on what their friends are doing, what their parents suggest, or what feels safe. This assessment is different. It is built on the same framework used by career counsellors worldwide — the Holland RIASEC model — and adapted specifically for Indian students and the Indian job market.

You will answer 70 honest questions across 7 areas: Practical Skills, Research and Analysis, Creative Expression, Helping People, Leadership and Business, Organisation and Systems, and your Subject Interests. At the end, you will receive a personalised report — with your name on it — that shows your top career directions, your natural strengths, the work environment that suits your personality, and a step-by-step roadmap for what to do next. Whether you are in Class 10, Class 12, or already in college, this report will give you clarity that most students never get.

  • 70 questions across 7 areas of interest
  • Personalised result with your name — not a generic report
  • Career directions organised by how well they match your profile
  • Subject-wise insights based on what you genuinely enjoy
  • Roadmap for Class 10, Class 12, graduation, and beyond
  • Market skills to build — specific to your career direction
